Process

How It Works

Step 01
Upload Forms
Upload your PDF forms or specify the form type. FAST supports multi-page forms, signatures, and complex layouts.
Step 02
Define Test Cases
Write test cases in plain English — no scripting, no code. Describe exactly what to check and the AI handles the rest.
Step 03
AI Validates
The AI engine analyses fields, formats, values, and visual layout. Gemini, GPT-4o, or Claude — your choice of model.
Step 04
Review & Approve
Review findings with your team, resolve or flag false positives, then approve through the full audit-trail workflow.
Platform

Built for Enterprise

Multi-Model AI
Switch between Gemini 2.5 Flash, GPT-4o, or Claude — or configure per-project. Never locked into one vendor.
Visual Diff Engine
Page-by-page pixel comparison with signature detection. Catch layout shifts, missing fields, and format regressions.
Jira Integration
One-click defect filing to Jira Cloud. Attach evidence screenshots, annotated PDFs, and classification details automatically.
Scheduled Runs
Set cron-style schedules to run validation automatically overnight or on every release. CI/CD-ready via webhook triggers.
Evidence Bundle
Export audit-ready ZIP bundles with annotated PDFs, diff images, and JSON summaries for compliance and sign-off.
Review Workflow
Assign findings to team members, mark defects or false positives, and gate approvals with a complete audit log.
